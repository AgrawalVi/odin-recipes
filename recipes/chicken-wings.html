Grilled Chicken Wings

Some juicy chicken wings that would make for the perfect dinner!

Nothing really competes with a nice platter of grilled chicken wings. The mix of juiciness with a nice crispy skin is pretty unmatched in the world of food. With the perfect marination mixed with the perfect sauce, it is bound to become anyone's favorite food. Best of all, this recipe ditches the deep frier so you will no longer have that post meal guilt.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 20 chicken wings (flats and drums separated)
  • Dipping sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat, around 350Â°F
  2. In a small bowl, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, and salt and pepper
  3. Fully coat each wing in the flour mixture
  4. Place the wings side by side on the grill
  5. Grill for 20 minutes, flipping the wings every 5 minutes
  6. And finally, serve with your favorite dipping sauce!
